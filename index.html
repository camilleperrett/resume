PROFILE

I am a young and excited Graphic Designer. I have graduated from a Graphic Design degree with a final mark of A- and have won a gold pin for my work in the 2015 Best Awards, student - graphic design category. My interests in the graphic design field lie in publication, photography, editorial, fashion, branding, and illustration. I am passionate about all the arts including art, food, fashion, and music.

KEY SKILLS AND QUALITIES
  • Graphic Design - I pride myself on my research ability and understanding of the design process. I am confident in using the programs InDesign CS5.5, Photoshop CS5.1, Lightroom 5 and Illustrator. I have some experience using Adobe After Effects. I am comfortable using both Mac and PC.

  • Photography - I have completed two courses in photographic practices at Unitec Institute of technology. This was an elective class along side my graphic design degree. The course included learning the mechanics of a camera, how to use in manual to create different results, photo editing in lightroom 5, studio equipment/setup and lighting.

  • People skills - Due to my time management and kind, patient manner, I am able to stay calm in a range of situations; able to defuse many problems.

  • Problem Solving - I do not let my emotions rule me and am very efficient under pressure. This helps me to think logically and will ask for help when needed.

  • Creativity - I have always been passionate in the arts and have dabbled in all sorts of mediums ranging from the hands on techniques of illustration, painting, sculpture, sewing, to more digital forms of photography, and graphic design.
